Who am I?

Personality

I do not only accept programming as a duty in a professional aspect, but also as a personal thing that complements me. When I get involved with a given task, especially if it contains programming, I do everything to get the desired result by achieving it with a lot of work.

For example, I worked on a project for three months and when I finally thought it was finished, new things came up. At this point, instead of giving up an unfinished work, I mobilized myself and solved the problem in order to fulfill the goals I set for myself.

Talking about myself, I would say that I am a sociable and talkative person with a good sense of humor. I enjoy going out with friends for a walk or playing games like table tennis, table football and pool.

In addition, I think that an active lifestyle is important for recharging the human body and thus for its productivity. I spend part of my free time on sport, where I can relax. My favorite sports are swimming and fitness.

Professionality

From professional point of view, I am a well-organized and hard-working person. The combination of these qualities makes me a person who does not stop before finishing the given tasks. Moreover, a person who can easily acclimatize to different circumstances and who is able to grasp new concepts quickly and efficiently.

For example, if the company I am working for decides to change the framework or programming language they use, I can easily and quickly adapt to the current situation and avoid any possible difficulties that might pop up.

One of my strongest professional characteristics is persistence, which means that when I have something to do I am trying to finish it as soon as possible. That does not mean that quality is not important for me. Contrariwise, quality is the most essential part of my work.

In my experience, I realized that keeping up with the new market trends means a lot to the client. Therefore, I seek to be active on social networks, to keep track of the actual articles about my profession as well as to follow the latest innovations in the field.

When working with clients, I am always trying to fulfill their needs as much as I can. I am flexible if the client's needs get changed and I can quickly switch to another way of working.

For example, if they have some kind of problem that just came up and I find out about it on time, it would not affect the deadline that has been specified. Furthermore, details are an important thing of the profession, so I prefer to keep everything clear before I start doing the project.
